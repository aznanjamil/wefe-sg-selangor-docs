# Finance

The **Finance** page helps you find ways to pay for water, energy, food, and environment projects in the Sungai Selangor basin.

> 📷 **Screenshot to capture:** `static/img/site/finance-overview.png`
>
> ![](/img/site/finance-overview.png)

## Why Financing Matters

The basin has a money problem:

- **0% spare water** for new users — everything is already allocated
- **2,934 MLD** of river water flows to the sea unused because there is not enough storage
- Old pipes waste **29.6%** of treated water (target: 20% by 2037)
- Forests, peat swamps, and mangroves need protection but nobody pays for them directly

Fixing these problems costs money. This page shows where that money can come from.

## Types of Financing

| Type | What It Is | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Public funding** | Government pays | Federal or state budgets for dams, pipes, treatment plants |
| **Private finance** | Companies or banks pay | Water utility bonds, private investors in infrastructure |
| **Blended finance** | Government + private together | Green bonds where public money reduces risk for private investors |
| **Nature-based finance** | Pay for nature's services | Farmers get paid to protect forests that keep river water clean |
| **Grants** | Free or cheap money | Climate adaptation funds, development bank loans |

## Step by Step: Using the Finance Finder Tool

### Step 1 — Open the Tool

Click **Finance Finder** in the sidebar or top navigation.

> ![](/img/site/finance-tool-open.png)

### Step 2 — Enter Your Project

Fill in the basic details:

| Field | What to Pick |
|-------|-------------|
| **Sector** | Water, Energy, Food, or Environment |
| **Scale** | Catchment (whole basin), Mukim (one area), or Urban (city) |
| **Type** | New infrastructure, efficiency upgrade, or nature-based solution |

### Step 3 — Apply Filters (Optional)

Narrow down the results:

| Filter | Use It When |
|--------|-------------|
| **Risk level** | You need low-risk options (e.g. government-backed) or can accept higher risk |
| **Timeline** | You need money fast (grants) or can wait for long-term loans |
| **Return needed** | Private investors want profit; public projects may not |

> ![](/img/site/finance-filters.png)

### Step 4 — Review Results

The tool shows financing options that match your project. Each result includes:

- **Who provides it** — Government, bank, green fund, etc.
- **How much** — Typical amount range
- **Terms** — Interest rate, payback period, conditions
- **Best for** — What kind of project this money suits

> ![](/img/site/finance-results.png)


**Tip:** If no filters are applied, all available instruments are shown.

> ![](/img/site/finance-all.png)
