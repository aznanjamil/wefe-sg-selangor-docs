# WEFE Nexus Manual

This is the user manual for **WEFE Nexus** — a research collaboration between **Monash University** and the **Selangor Climate Adaptation Center (SCAC)** for analyzing the Sungai Selangor river basin.

## What WEFE Nexus is for

One river basin supplies about **60% of the drinking water for Kuala Lumpur and Selangor** — and it's already fully spoken for, with no spare drop left for new users. Billions of litres still slip past to the sea each year.

WEFE Nexus reads the basin as a single **Water-Energy-Food-Environment** system, looking for where smarter management — not just new dams — can unlock the water it already has.

If you're a planner who came here to find a specific mukim, go straight to **TE Atlas → Getting Started**.
